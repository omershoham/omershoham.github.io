Privacy Policy — Shir Analytics

Last updated: July 2026

Shir Analytics is a personal, non-commercial analytics tool used by a single creator to view statistics about her own social media accounts (Instagram and TikTok).

What data we access

With the account owner's explicit authorization via the platforms' official APIs, the tool accesses: profile statistics (follower counts, likes, video counts) and per-post performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares) of the owner's own account only.

How data is used and stored

Data is stored locally on the account owner's private computer and used solely to generate private analytics reports for the account owner. It is not sold, shared, published, or transferred to any third party.

Data of other users

The tool does not collect personal data about other users. Public comments on the owner's own posts may be processed in aggregate (e.g., counting questions) for the owner's private content planning.

Data deletion

All stored data can be deleted at any time by the account owner by deleting the local database. To revoke access, the account owner can remove the app's authorization in their Instagram or TikTok settings.

Contact

Questions: contact the account owner via the linked social profiles.
